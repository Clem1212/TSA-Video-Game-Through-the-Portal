Hansel and Gretel reach the top of the beanstalk, gazing at the mystical world above.

Suddenly Rapunzel and Robin Hood appear.

Rapunzel: "Welcome, travelers. You've made it further than most."

Robin Hood: "The Witch is hunting us, capturing our stories to rewrite our fates."

Rapunzel: "We can help. We’ve fought this battle for years,

and we can train you to do the same."

Robin Hood: "You’ve proven your strength. Now, let us teach you how to fight back."

Hansel and Gretel exchange a determined glance.

Gretel: "We’ll join you. We’ll stop her."

as the group heads toward them they get ready for what lies ahead.
