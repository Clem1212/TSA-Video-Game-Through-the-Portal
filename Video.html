The plot revolves around Hansel and Gretel, who escape into a magical portal and get pulled into different fairy tale worlds, battling a wicked witch along the way. They meet famous characters like Rapunzel and Robin Hood, who help them develop new skills to fight the witch. The game is designed around the shown narrative, with different mini-games inspired by various fairy tales and various retro games. Here’s a breakdown of how it plays out:

Main Story: Hansel and Gretel stumble upon a magical book in the witch’s candy house and are transported to other fairy tales. They must defeat the witch and beat the different games to return home.

Mini-Games: Each fairy tale has its own game, such as: Pied Piper: Avoid rats and collect eggs, activating powers to progress. Golden Goose: Dodge enemies and collect eggs, using power-ups. Aladdin: Fly while avoiding clouds, aiming for a score of 20 points. Jack and the Beanstalk: Jump on blocks to climb the beanstalk and reach the top. Robin Hood Training:Learn how to shoot targets for witch combat training. Rapunzel: Players must learn how to avoid falling items to win. Witch Boss Fight: Players must avoid spells and collect book pieces to win and defeat the witch.

Rules: The Pied Piper Press SPACE to view rules. Use arrow keys/WASD to move. Avoid rats, collect eggs. Press DOWN or S to activate powers. Collect book pages to move to the next level.

Golden Goose Press SPACE to view rules. Use arrow keys/WASD to move. Dodge enemies, collect eggs. Use power-ups (Press SPACE).

Aladdin Press SPACE to view rules. Use W/UP arrow to fly. Avoid clouds, reach 20 points to win.

Jack and the Beanstalk Press SPACE to view rules. Use W/UP arrow to jump. Jump on blocks to climb the beanstalk. Reach the top to win.

Hansel - Robin Hood Training Press SPACE to view rules. Use arrow keys to move. Click/move mouse to shoot targets. Train with Robin Hood for witch fight.

Gretel - Rapunzel Training Use LEFT/RIGHT keys to dodge falling items. Press DOWN to activate shield. Train with Rapunzel for witch fight.

Witch Boss Fight Press SPACE to view rules. Use arrow keys/WASD to move. Run from the witch, collect book pieces. Climb platforms, avoid witch spells, and collect eggs. Find the full book to fight the witch.

The games feature simple controls (using arrow keys, WASD, and sometimes the mouse), and the players need to perform tasks like dodging, collecting items, or activating special powers to move on to the next level or story.

The overall theme emphasize teamwork, friendship, and growing stronger by working together—something Hansel and Gretel learn through their journey. The combination of narrative and interactive gameplay makes this an exciting adventure!
